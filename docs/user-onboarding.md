# User Onboarding

How a new club member starts using the Rez booking bot.

No account creation or server-side setup is needed — members are self-provisioned on first contact.

---

## Step 1 — Install Telegram

Download and install Telegram from [telegram.org](https://telegram.org). Create an account if you don't have one yet.

## Step 2 — Find the booking bot

Ask your facility admin for the bot name or link, or search for it in Telegram (e.g. `@EtcEnBookingBot`).

## Step 3 — Start a conversation

Tap **Start** (or send `/start`). The bot will introduce itself.

## Step 4 — Make a booking

Just write naturally:

> "Book court 1 tomorrow at 6pm for Mike"

> "I'd like to reserve a court on Saturday morning"

The bot confirms the booking and tells you which court and time was reserved.

## Step 5 — Check the calendar

Your booking appears on the facility's shared calendar within seconds of the bot's confirmation. Ask your facility admin for the calendar link.

---

## Notes

- **No account needed** — your Telegram identity is used directly.
- **Natural language** — no commands or menus, just describe what you want.
- **Cancellations** — ask the bot to cancel by reservation ID: "Cancel reservation abc12345"
